About

As a chemist, I used to work in the biotech field of DNA sequencing and protein purification. My career change into web development is a very exciting life path for me. Web development requires me to think outside of the box and come up with solutions to suit the client's needs. This field is extremely dynamic and presents developers with new types of problems and solutions. Falling into a rut of "same old same old" doesn't come into play here. I enjoy utilizing my creative and technical skills to produce a product. Creative skills apply to problem solving to find solutions, not just design. As a web developer, I look forward to tackling any challenges presented to me and being part of a productive team.

I have a solid background in programming and scripting languages. The scripting languages including HTML, CSS, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, PHP, and MySQL. For programming, I have completed coursework in C++. In addition, I use two very powerful programs for graphic design, Photoshop and Illustrator.

Currently, I am a proud participant in WebLab. WebLab is an intensive 12 week web development boot camp that is based in Fort Collins, Colorado. Taught by highly knowledgeable web development professionals, the course provides targeted training in Front End Development, including CSS, HTML, JavaScript and UI/UX Design. Prior to participating in WebLab, I had completed all of the coursework mentioned above at a college. WebLab served to solidify my foundation and to provide me with tool sets vital to today's web developers such as node.js, AJAX, Github, Bootstrap, understanding what UX is, responsive web site design, frameworks, wire-framing, API's, Webstorm IDE, and optimizing website performance. Many of these skills are not available in regular classroom instruction. I look forward to demonstrating my newly acquired skills with you.
